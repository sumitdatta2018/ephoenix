MISSION & VISSION
  1. To provide all type of education to all student and also everyone at west bengal to everywhere.
  2. To acquire, establish computer education school,books-banks, free education school, aduit literacy centers,youth clubs etc; backward old and child literacy centre.

  5. To help physically &mentally handicapped people,the old &the diseases men in every possible waY.
  6. To organized sports and games for the young people.
  7. Vocational computer training to backward and general class children.
